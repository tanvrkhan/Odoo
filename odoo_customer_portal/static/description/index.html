Odoo customer Portal

"Simplify purchase management to Odoo"

Stand alone customer management solution for you. To reduce dependencies on email communication and delay in correspondence, assign individual portals to customers in their Odoo website accounts. So, you can send RFQs directly to the customer portal and communicate instantly. And customers can check the status of their quotations from the customer portal.
